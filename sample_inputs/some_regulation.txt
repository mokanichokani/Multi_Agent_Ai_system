Subject: New Data Privacy Regulation Update - GDPR Compliance

Effective January 1st, 2025, all companies handling EU citizen data must adhere to the updated General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) addendum 7.2.
This includes stricter consent management protocols and mandatory data breach notifications within 48 hours.
Non-compliance can result in fines up to 4% of global annual turnover.
